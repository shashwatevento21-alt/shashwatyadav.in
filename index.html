About Me

From Founder to Performance Marketer

I started my journey as the founder of Eventoplanner, a service business that I scaled to ₹30L+ in revenue over 4+ years. During that time, I wasn't just running ads—I was on the ground, speaking with customers, evaluating lead quality, and making ROI-based decisions.

This hands-on experience taught me something most marketers never learn: the difference between vanity metrics and business outcomes. I understand the pressure of payroll, customer expectations, and revenue targets—not just campaign metrics.

Today, I bring that business-first mindset to performance marketing. I manage ₹5.7L+ monthly ad spend across Google and Meta Ads for commercial real estate and healthcare clients, with a focus on lead quality, cost efficiency, and measurable results.

My approach combines operational experience with technical execution—I don't just run campaigns, I think about how they impact the bottom line.
